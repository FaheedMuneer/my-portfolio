My Projects

Project Name: A Novel high Step-up DC/DC converter based on Integrating coupled Inductor and Switched capacitor Techniques for Renewable Energy Applications.

Brief description: The suggested converter will be suitable for DG systems based on Renewable energy sources which acquire high step-up voltage transfer gain. In this converter, the energy stored in the leakage inductance is recycled to improve the performance. Furthermore, votage stress on the main power switch is reduced. Therefore, a switch with a low on-state resistance can be chosen. the steady-state operation of the converter has been analyzed in detail. Finally, with this converter, a hardware prototype is implemented which converts 40-V input voltage into 400-V output voltage.
